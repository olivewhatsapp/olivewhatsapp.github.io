Cosmic Wonders

Custom cards for the web series by PM Seymour. Main set has 69 cards (nice) plus tokens and alternate arts. The 12-card bonus sheet CosWon: Pre-Reboot is also complete, whereas supplementary sets Shooting Star and Path to Wonderland are works in progress. New mechanics: Celestial, Insatiable, Attunement, Planet tokens, Rivalry, Momentum.

SHR

Likely to consist of two Commander precons, a WURG tap/untap deck and a UBR artifacts deck. Nobles are a prevalen creature type. New mechanics: Unite, Machinery tokens.

Omenpath Gateways

A bottom-up set that contains designs prioritising interesting effects over flavorful designs. Updates are infrequent, and a lot of the cards are not yet properly balanced.

Other Cards

Custom arts, joke arts, and card designs that don't otherwise fit into a different section. Also contains my submissions to the Omenpaths Flavoring Project.
